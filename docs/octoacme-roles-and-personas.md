# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers coordinate the release lifecycle, from planning and validation through deployment and rollback scenarios. They ensure releases are stable, timely, and aligned with organizational standards.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate release schedules and deployment windows
- Validate pre-release criteria (testing, security, documentation)
- Manage rollback and mitigation plans
- Facilitate communication between development, QA, and operations
- Track release metrics and post-release health

### Goals
- Deliver high-quality releases with minimal disruption
- Reduce time-to-production while maintaining stability
- Ensure clear rollback strategies and incident readiness

### Typical Communication
- Release status updates and deployment timelines
- Pre-release checklists and go/no-go decisions
- Post-release summaries and incident reports

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers define user workflows, create wireframes, and establish usability standards. They ensure the product is intuitive, accessible, and aligned with user needs.

### Responsibilities
- Design user interfaces, wireframes, and prototypes
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Define interaction patterns and design systems
- Collaborate with product and engineering on implementation feasibility
- Review and validate designs in production

### Goals
- Create delightful, accessible user experiences
- Ensure design consistency across the product
- Balance user needs with technical and business constraints

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- User research findings and usability reports
- Wireframes, mockups, and design specifications

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads advise on security controls, conduct security reviews, and coordinate incident response. They work across all teams to ensure the product meets security standards and compliance requirements.

### Responsibilities
- Define security requirements and controls
- Review code, infrastructure, and third-party dependencies for vulnerabilities
- Conduct threat modeling and risk assessments
- Coordinate security incident response and remediation
- Provide security training and awareness

### Goals
- Minimize security risks and vulnerabilities
- Ensure compliance with security policies and regulations
- Foster a security-conscious culture

### Typical Communication
- Security review findings and recommendations
- Threat assessments and incident reports
- Security standards and best practice guidance

---

## Customer Success / Support

### Role Summary
Customer Success and Support teams represent the voice of the customer, provide feedback on product usability, and ensure successful customer adoption. They validate feature releases and contribute to post-release success.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and communicate customer feedback and pain points
- Validate product features from a user perspective
- Provide input on rollout strategies and documentation
- Support incident triage and customer communication
- Participate in retrospectives to share customer insights

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Ensure smooth feature adoption and onboarding
- Reduce support escalations through proactive input

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback summaries and feature requests
- Support ticket trends and common issues
- Release impact assessments and documentation needs

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts define measurement plans, analyze product and technical metrics, and provide actionable insights. They enable data-driven decision-making across product, engineering, and leadership.

### Responsibilities
- Define KPIs and success metrics for features
- Build dashboards and reports for product and technical metrics
- Analyze user behavior, performance, and business impact
- Provide data-driven recommendations to product and leadership
- Validate hypotheses through A/B testing and experimentation

### Goals
- Enable evidence-based product decisions
- Identify trends, opportunities, and risks through data
- Improve measurement and observability capabilities

### Typical Communication
- Metric definitions and measurement plans
- Analysis reports and insight presentations
- Dashboard reviews and data quality assessments
